          Classes & Races

          Dark Age of Camelot offers players with a grand total of 21 races and 45 classes across all three realms. Each realm has seven playable character races available for players to use with a grand total of 19 races (each realm has access to the Minotaur race), although they are restricted to the classes they have access to.
